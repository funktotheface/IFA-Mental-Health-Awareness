Talk About Your Feelings

It’s okay to feel sad, mad, or happy – everyone has different emotions! If something is bothering you, talk to a grown-up or a friend. Sharing how you feel helps you feel better and understand your emotions more clearly.

Take Deep Breaths

When you feel upset or stressed, try taking deep breaths. Breathe in slowly through your nose, hold it for a moment, then breathe out slowly. It helps calm your body and mind, making it easier to think clearly and feel better..

Take Time for Play

Playing isn’t just fun—it’s also good for your brain! Whether you’re drawing, running, or playing with toys, spending time doing things you enjoy can help you relax and feel happy.

Get Enough Sleep

Sleep is like a reset button for your body and mind. Make sure to get plenty of sleep each night so you feel rested and ready to face the day. A good night’s sleep helps you stay happy, healthy, and focused.
